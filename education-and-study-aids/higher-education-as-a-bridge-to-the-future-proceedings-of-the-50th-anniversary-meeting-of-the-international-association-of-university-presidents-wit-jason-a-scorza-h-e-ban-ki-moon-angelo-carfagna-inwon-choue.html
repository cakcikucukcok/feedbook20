This volume of essays and speeches by noted international higher education leaders explores the critical role of higher education both as an active part of global civil society and as a foundation for the realization of a just, peaceful, and prosperous global future. The collection contains the proceedings of the 50th Annual Meeting of the International Association of University Presidents (IAUP), the world’s largest organization of higher education leaders.
