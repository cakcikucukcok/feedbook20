Healing a Broken World is about taking the steps necessary for us to come together to solve the world's problems, and ensure a future for our children and our children's children. Topics covered in Healing A Broken World include: The challenges an economic model that is based on consumption has for the sustainability of the planet. The challenges increasing globalization and cultural diversity create for our existing values and beliefs. The breakdown of trust in our leaders, and in their ability to deal with the increasing political, religious, economic, racial, and cultural tensions we are being confronted with. The steps we need to take, and the sacrifices we need to be willing to make, to restore trust and cooperation and begin solving the world's problems.
