We have a tendency to take the positive aspects of our lives for granted, and view the negative experiences as destructive, therefore the negative events instead of being evaluated and understood are quickly rejected and the valuable lessons that they portray are lost. Joseph Trevinos new book I Remember Yesterday are the memoirs of an individual who has experience a lot of good and negative thing in his life, and has developed the knowledge that events in our lives do not happen in a random fashion. People we meet, things that we do, places we visit, pleasant and negative events that we experience, all are a part of the master plan in our lives that determine our destiny. So many lessons to learn if we would only take the time to do so. Events depicted in this effort are authentic and by no means a figment of the authors imagination. They are convincing evidence there is a Guardian for each and every one of us that watches over us and on numerous occasions has led us from harms way. The role that we play during the cycle of life is important for all of us. Consider the numerous lives that we touch as we make our journey and the effect that we have on others.
