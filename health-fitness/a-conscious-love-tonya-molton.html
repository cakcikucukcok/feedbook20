Self-love is the beginning of any good relationship and is vital to all connections. Self-love is the breath of life to a relationship. What you put into any association is what you will get out of it. Love is the key to living a full life.

A Conscious Love, by author Tonya Molton, offers an interactive daily awareness journal revolving around the theme of love. Each selection offers a spiritual-based discussion of love, relationships, life, and living. It provides little pearls of wisdom and asks life-changing questions which promote conscious thinking about love. Space at the end of each chapter allows you to chart daily your thoughts and growth.

In A Conscious Love, Molton communicates that love isnt easy so we must consciously make an effort and put in the work to grow any relationship of love, the love of a spouse, a partner, a child, a family member, or simply a friendship.
