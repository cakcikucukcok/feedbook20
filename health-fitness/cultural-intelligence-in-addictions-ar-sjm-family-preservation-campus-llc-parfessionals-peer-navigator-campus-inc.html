The course workbook contains the following:

Learning Journal. Activities throughout the course meant to challenge you, increase self-awareness, and prepare you for working as a Peer Recovery Navigator.

Student Practicum Activities. Activities meant to supplement learning throughout the practicum experience. The activities suggested in this guide may be amended, changed, added to or substituted as students and supervisors see fit. The goal is to provide a starting point for real-world application of skills, but it is far better for a student and supervisor to choose activities that are most appropriate and helpful to the work they are doing. Get creative! Customize them and make them your own.

Practicum Writing Assignment. A journal activity related to the student practicum activity that assists students in documenting the practicum experience and showing the application of course concepts.
