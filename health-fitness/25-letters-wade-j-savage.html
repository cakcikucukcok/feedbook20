25 Letters is an affirmation of love and devotion between a husband and wife. After losing his job, Wade had to reconnect and reaffirm his relationship with God and his wife. His moment of clarity came during the Christmas season of 2009. As a simple act of love for his wife, Wade decided to write a letter everyday to his wife. Along with each letter he also gave her a small gift in order to show the depth of his love for her. While reconnecting with his wife, Wade also reconnected with his Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. Each letter was a blessing that further fortified his marriage and made God the head of their household.
