Her pro athlete husband cheated on her—and she’s not sure she’ll ever be ready to give her heart again. Now single and working at a children’s charity, it’s not just event planning that has her putting in overtime. Local football celebrity Michael Jones is organizing a gala affair with Natalie. With the vulnerable beauty arousing his fiercest, protective instinct—and a desire neither has the power to resist–Michael is ready to let his playboy past go. If Natalie’s willing, together they can surrender to their future.
