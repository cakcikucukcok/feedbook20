Do you believe in angels? Are they cutesy and pretty, or are they powerful energetic beings? This book of artwork will help you get a glimpse of angels and other luminous beings through the eyes of Nami Nesterowicz, a former skeptic whose near-death experience caused him to see the world in a whole different way. After working in hospice, physical therapy, and massage therapy, his experience helped him to become a healer who can see, feel, and hear angelic energy. He creates paintings in order to pass that energy on to others. He now gets commissions for his artwork all over the world, and paints the energy of his clients and that of the beings surrounding them. The paintings continue to change after they are painted and often contain hidden images that emerge later.

See this 26-piece collection of Nami's artwork and feel the vibration of living paintings.
