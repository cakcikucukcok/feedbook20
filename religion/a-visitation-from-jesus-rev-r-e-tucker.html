Dr. Richard E. Tucker has been ministering as an Evangelist since 1954 and has Pastored Churches for 31 years. He is an Ordained and Licensed Minister with the Assemblies of God and was Senior Pastor of Calvary Assembly of God Church in Stanton, California for 24 years.

He graduated with a Bachelor of Theology in 1980 from California Graduate School of Theology in Glendale, California and completed his Post-Graduate work at International Bible College and Seminary where he earned his D.D. in 1983.

His field of expertise is in Pneumatology and he has taught in Seminaries both in the United Sates and internationally on current events in Eschatology. In his spare time he dabbles in inventions of all kinds. He resides in Orange County, California.
