Master Ryuho Okawa, author of over 1,600 books and spiritual leader with passionate followers in over 100 countries around the world, shows us another miracle by calling out his own guardian spirit. 

He has been taking on different roles as the world teacher, giving crucial advice in politics and economy like the following: predicting the confusion in the EU, advice on how to deal with the Lehman Shock, warning against the rise of China and also advice to take an aggressive monetary easing approach, which became the first phase of Abenomics - all while continuing to give guidance as a spiritual leader. 

In this book, Master Okawa summons Shakyamuni Buddha, which is his own past life and also his guardian consciousness. Shakyamuni Buddha was also a great religious leader and a national teacher who gave his king political advice. Various things such as the main principle for building an ideal future society, the ideal form of religion and politics in respect to each other, and the conditions for democracy to function properly are disclosed. 

Spiritual Messages from the Guardian Spirit of Ryuho Okawa - The Divine Voice of Shakyamuni Buddha - is the compass to guide you through an age of chaos.
