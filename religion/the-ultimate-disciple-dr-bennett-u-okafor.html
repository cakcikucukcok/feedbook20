Focus on Becoming THE ULTIMATE DISCIPLE

Christ told Martha, One thing is needful (Luke 10:38-42).

David desired one thing from the Lord: One thing have I desired from the Lord, and that will I seek after (Psalm 27:4).

Paul said to the Philippians, But this one thing I do (Philippians 3:13).

All these one things crystallize into ONE BIG THING: TO KNOW CHRIST AND TO BE MADE CONFORMABLE INTO HIS IMAGE AND LIKENESS. This is the purpose of our lives.

This book is written to help us attain to this divine purpose. It reveals twenty-eight great attributes of Christs life for every believer to emulate through yielding to the Spirit, so we may fully resemble Christ. It will help you understand more clearly the character of Christ, and as you make Him your model, the end is eternal life.

I highly recommend this great book which clearly reveals the nature and character of our Lord Jesus Christ so that we can all emulate HimIt will be a wonderful companion for you along the way.

The Reverend Professor Paul Emeka

General Superintendent

Assemblies of God, Nigeria.

The Ultimate Disciple should attract worldwide distribution because of its relevance and timeliness.
