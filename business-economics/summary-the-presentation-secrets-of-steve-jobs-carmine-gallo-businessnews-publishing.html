Complete summary of Carmine Gallo's book: "The Presentation Secrets of Steve Jobs" 

The review of the ideas in Carmine Gallo's book: "The Presentation Secrets of Steve Jobs" shows that Jobs was a magnetic pitchman who sold his ideas with flair and charisma. But behind the scenes, he worked hard at methodically improving his presentations. This summary highlights that anyone can learn to present like Steve Jobs. All you need to do is to take the presentation template he used and then add your own planning and practice efforts. Charisma is not a prerequisite, but hard work and systematic preparation most certainly are.

Added-value of this summary: • Save time• Understand key concepts • Expand your business knowledge

To learn more, read "The Presentation Secrets of Steve Jobs" and use the techniques that will help you convince your audience.
