Agni arrives. Agni is the Superhero of Health. Using her special powers, Agni is able to cure Om from a dangerous food allergy.

Inspired by an experience with a pediatric patient in her Urgent Care Clinic, Dr. Madhavi Reddy wrote Agni to educate children and their parents about allergic reactions and how to manage this specific situation. Dr. Reddy hopes to relieve fears children may have regarding doctors, injections, and ambulances.
