¿Have I ever told you the story of Pier?

Pier was a boy with a mouse nose, dreamy look, and hedgehog hair. He believed he could do anything he set out to be, he was that innocent.

—I would like to jump like the jumpstones— he said one day, almost without Knowing why, loud enough for others to hear.

His mother, Crossbow, smiled as mothers do; Spring, his father, coughed as parents cough; and the grandfather shook his head up and down.
