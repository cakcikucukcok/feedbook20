Sheesh off the Leash! is  a fun and exciting adventure, for children of all ages,  as Sheesh leads the reader on in this humorous dog story. What an adorable heroI With broad appeal and reach beyond the urban-based setting for this charming, and most appealingly illustrated tale.

A focus group of Moms said the following: 

"Children often feel small in a big world and most certainly will identify with a little dog lost on a busy street!" 

"Sheesh's fantasies picturing himself as a  magician, a lion, and Superman are among those I see playing out daily in my children's lives!" 

Volunteering for  The Good Dog Foundation  , with her dog Emmett, proved to be a wonderful platform for Ms. Mahanna as students had the opportunity to read Sheesh in their reading program. The lively discussions around the issues Sheesh faces in the book, (wandering away from your parent, taking care to not let your dog off a leash, and what they would have done in Sheesh’s situation) were proof that Sheesh makes excellent educational material for teachers and caretakers. 

In the spirit of picture books like Skippy Jon Jones and Motor Dog, this charmingly illustrated book is sure to become an often-requested read for young readers!
