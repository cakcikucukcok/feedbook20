Join Circle and Triangle on an adventure full of fun!

Enjoy demonstrating the principles of saving and sharing with this fun book.

Experience the joys of sunny days (a metaphor for when things go well) only to be met by the shock of a rainy day (a metaphor for the unexpected).

Together with your child, discover various intricacies illustrated throughout the book, including: concepts, numbers, shapes, colors and animals.

Kids are very impressionable. Adverse effects may include and are not limited to finding bits of cookies in odd places.

What's included: Colored story book and coloring version at the end.
