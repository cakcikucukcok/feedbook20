Children never forget their first dog, and it's very likely they won't forget Pup Pup Gets Adopted, either! This charming, realistic depiction of one family's canine adoption experience is a fun and engaging way to teach children, ages 3 to 6, about the rewards and responsibilities connected to adopting a family dog. Pup Pup is an adorable German shepherd puppy who just happens to attract the attention of little Scarlett when she visits a German shepherd's young brood with her parents and Brother Zak. The puppy that is running around chasing his tail is the one that Scarlett singles out. Pup Pup Gets Adopted is an educational, fun way to tap into children's natural love for animals and is especially useful for parents with children interested in adopting a first pet.
