Little Rosie Posie has been living with her grammy and pops since she was a baby. Her mom died when she was born and her dad could not handle raising her, so her grammy and pops adopt her. Grammy and Pops had long since dreamed of retiring, buying an RV, and traveling across the United States. Now, they have a little baby to raise. So they decide to take Little Rosie Posie traveling with them in the RV. Now they can raise their granddaughter and live their dream. Little Rosie does not go to a school like most boys and girls do. Since she travels with her grandparents, the world is her classroom. Her pops is a professional photographer who sells his photographs to magazine companies for everyone to enjoy of all the places that they visit. Her grammy is a retired schoolteacher and now she is Little Rosies private teacher.
